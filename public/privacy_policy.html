Privacy Policy for Milestone Web Service

1. Introduction

Welcome to Milestone, a free web service that allows users to interact with the Strava API. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following user data:

  • Strava User ID (athleteID): Your unique Strava user identifier.
  • Access Token: A secure token used to authenticate with the Strava API.
  • Athlete Name: Your name as provided on Strava.
  • Athlete Profile Picture: A link to your Strava profile picture.
  • Athlete Username: Your Strava username.
  • Enable Bike Description: A setting to enable or disable bike-related descriptions.
  • Enable Run Description: A setting to enable or disable running-related descriptions.
  • Enable Description Changes: A setting to allow or disallow description changes.
  • Refresh Token: A secure token used to refresh the access token.

We do not sell any of this data to third parties.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with a personalized and enhanced experience on Milestone.
  • To automatically add descriptions to your Strava activities based on your preferences.
  • To improve our web service and ensure it functions correctly.
  • To provide customer support and address any issues or concerns you may have.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. This includes the use of encryption and secure data storage practices.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on Milestone. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device and help us remember your session information, so you don't have to log in every time you visit our website.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccuracies in your data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
